\#\#\# 1.1 The Meaning of "Chith"  
In colloquial Kannada, "Chith" (ಚಿತ್) denotes a state of absolute obliteration—being completely wrecked, passed out, or knocked unconscious after consuming an excessive amount of alcohol. This is the visual foundation of the studio. The website must not look like a literal party or nightlife flyer. Instead, it must translate the \*physical sensations\* of intoxication into cutting-edge digital physics:  
\*   \*\*The Blur:\*\* Visual distortion and spatial disorientation.  
\*   \*\*The Weight:\*\* Gravitational heaviness and sluggish, drag-induced momentum.  
\*   \*\*The Clarity:\*\* Sudden, sharp, high-contrast snaps back into crisp reality.

\#\#\# 1.2 The Origin Story (The Park Bench)  
The studio was forged by a collective of cinephiles in their late 20s. Lacking industry backing or capital, their creative incubator was an empty park bench. They spent hours sitting there doing absolutely "nothing" but intensely analyzing world cinema, debating scripts, and pooling personal cash to fund their first short film.   
\*   \*\*The Design Goal:\*\* The website is the digital continuation of that park bench. It represents the specific transition where doing "nothing" on a bench transforms into making independent cinema.

\#\#\# 1.3 Auteur Influences (Atmospheric DNA)  
While the site must feature zero direct image or text references to outside films, the underlying tension must feel familiar to fans of gritty, atmospheric, psychological cinema:  
\*   The oppressive, geometric symmetry and architectural dread of \*The Shining\*.

**4\. INTERACTIVE PSYCHOLOGY & MOTION DESIGN**

### **4.1 The Friction Principle**

Every animation must feel like it has physical mass. It should move through wet cement or thick liquid, not a frictionless digital vacuum.

* **The Acceleration Curve:** Use custom cubic-bezier curves for transitions that scale slow-to-fast-to-slow (`cubic-bezier(0.25, 1, 0.5, 1)`).

### **4.2 The "Altered Perception" Loop**

The site operates on an internal state machine called the `PerceptionEngine`.

* **Sober State:** Mouse movement is slow, scroll position is steady. The UI is clean, sharp, aligned perfectly to the 12-column grid.  
* **Intoxicated State:** Mouse movement spikes in velocity, or fast scrolling is registered. The UI undergoes immediate structural changes: lines bend, colors bleed, text shears up to 15 degrees, and text characters flicker erratically.  
* **The Hangover (Recovery):** When mouse movement or scrolling ceases, the interface smoothly glides back into alignment over a duration of exactly 1.2 seconds using linear interpolation (lerp).
