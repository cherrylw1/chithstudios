CHITH // 001

We are a collective of cinephiles in our late 20s who spent too many nights on broken park benches, doing absolutely nothing but talking about movies. No industry connections. No studio backing. Just an aggressive obsession with the medium and a shared pocket of pooled cash.

CHITH is our state of being. Obliterated by the weight of storytelling. Wrecked by the frames that linger long after the projector bulb goes cold. We don't make safe compromises, and we don't build polite portfolios. We build visceral, atmospheric, psychological spaces.

This is independent cinema born from total chaos, executed with cold mathematical precision.
